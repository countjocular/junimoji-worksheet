Junimoji Workout Space

Junimojis created by Friz Frizzle
Junimoji Workout Space by Johnnie (source)

You are making a crossword using the letters provided. The empty, black squares are not given - you will need to deduce them. The completed crossword will be in a valid grid, i.e., all words will be in the dictionary and the grid will be symmetrical. For each of the nine 3x3 subgrids you have been given the letters that go there, in the order they appear, going from left to right, top to bottom.

  1. Fill in the clues column on the right with the clues from your chosen junimoji. You can find some example puzzles here if you don't have one already.
  2. Click any square and type the letter of your choice.
  3. Double-click any square to set it as black. Double-click it again to set it as white. Double-click a third time to undo.
